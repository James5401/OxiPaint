> **The ultimate dark mode experience.** 8 intelligent strategies, smart element rounding, and unlimited custom palettes.

Ultra Dark Mode (UDM) is a powerful, highly customizable userscript designed to bring a premium dark mode experience to every corner of the web. Unlike simple inversion filters, UDM uses advanced heuristics and site-specific patches to ensure readability and visual harmony.

---

## ✨ Key Features

- **🚀 8 Intelligent Strategies:** Choose from `variables`, `heuristic`, `selective`, `layer`, `override`, `invert`, and more to find the perfect fit for any website.
- **🎨 Unlimited Custom Palettes:** Comes with beautiful presets (Apple, Dracula, Nord, Gruvbox) and allows you to create your own.
- **🖼️ Smart Element Handling:** Intelligent rounding of buttons, cards, and inputs; automatic dimming of bright images and backgrounds.
- **📄 PDF Renderer:** Deep dark mode support for the built-in PDF viewers (Edge/Chrome).
- **⚙️ Full Dashboard & Compact Panel:** A beautiful, Apple-style UI for managing global settings and per-site overrides.
- **⏰ Smart Scheduling:** Automatically toggle dark mode based on the time of day or sync with your system's OS theme.
- **🛠️ Element Picker:** Easily exclude specific elements from being affected by the dark mode.
